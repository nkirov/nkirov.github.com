Department: Computer Science

Competencies of the graduates:
    The main focus is on computing and networking technologies. The program is designed to offer both a broad-based education in technologies and a general multidisciplinary education. In addition to the core courses in computers, programming, networking technologies, and related project work, students study math, physics and communications. Together these courses will provide students with a solid and well-rounded framework on which to build a career.

Possibilities for job:
    Graduates could be doing work in any of the following areas:

  • Building network infrastructure and internet working
  • Architecture design and usability testing
  • Software development for businesses
  • Database and web applications development
  • Design of web-based training courses (instructional design) ?

    •     Students could be working for large telecom companies, software development companies, any company or organization requiring network support, design and management. And they could be doing this work in government, business or the non-profit sector - in virtually any sector of the economy.
