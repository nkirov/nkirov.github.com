Number Types

  • Use the asterisk * for multiplication (not dot or cross).
  • Comments are denoted by /* comment text */
    • Ignored by the compiler.
    • Explains the program to other programmers or yourself.
  • Variable naming rules:
  • Names must start with a letter.
    • Remaining characters are letters or underscore (_) (no spaces or special symbols)
    • You cannot use reserved words.
    • Variable names are case-sensitive.
