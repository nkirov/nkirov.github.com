The Bulgarian Collegiate Programming Contests

Republican Programming Olympiad for University Students (Републиканска студентска олимпиада по програмиране)

From 15 years until now, every year in our country is held National Bulgarian Collegiate Programming Contest (Republican Programming Olympiad for University students) -  BCPC. The beginning of such a competition was launched in 199 ... year, conducted a few years ago, and after a break, the competition was revived in 2000 under the name "Interuniversity Programming Tournament" (IPT). In 2005 the organizers changed the name to "Republican Programming Olympiad for University students".

BCPC is the only national programming contest for university students. It is an independent event organized by the participants - Bulgarian universities which offer courses in computer science and programming. The rules are close to those of ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest. They have changed and approved by the participants  BCPC is held every year in May and is organized by a Bulgarian University - host of the Olympics.

Each participating university is represented by one or more teams. Each team consists of three contestants and a coach. The official ranking includes only the best team of each university in the scoreboard of the testing system (team standings).
